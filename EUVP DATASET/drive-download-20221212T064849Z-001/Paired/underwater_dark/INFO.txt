Dataset name: Underwater_dark
Nature: a paired collection of images on EUVP dataset
Source: https://irvlab.dl.umn.edu/resources/euvp-dataset


Statistics
-----------
5550 pairs of images (in trainA and trainB folders) for training
  - trainA contain poor quality (or gray) images
  - trainB contain enhanced (or colored) images
  - same filenames for each pair

Another 570 images are for validation

----------------------------
* A total of 11,670 images
* Total file size: 199 MB
