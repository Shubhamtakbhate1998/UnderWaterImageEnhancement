Dataset name: Underwater_scenes
Nature: a paired collection of images on EUVP dataset
Source: https://irvlab.dl.umn.edu/resources/euvp-dataset


Statistics
-----------
2185 pairs of images (in trainA and trainB folders) for training
  - trainA contain poor quality images
  - trainB contain enhanced (or better quality) images
  - same filenames for each pair

Another 130 images are for validation
